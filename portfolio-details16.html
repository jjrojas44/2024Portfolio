Case Study:

Keep Going First Aid
Brief

Keep Going First Aid is a streamlined app that empowers users to handle emergencies confidently, providing quick access to essential first aid information, interactive tutorials, and emergency contacts—all designed for ease of use in urgent situations.

Project

Mobile App Concept

Role

Solo Design

Time

8 Week Project

Value Added

Keep Going First Aid is an intuitive app designed to empower users to respond effectively in emergencies. It provides quick access to essential first aid information, interactive tutorials, and emergency contacts, all structured to minimize response time and maximize user confidence when it matters most. Designed with a user-first approach, the app’s easy navigation and practical resources ensure first aid guidance is always just a tap away.

Problem Statement

In emergency situations, people often struggle to access critical first aid information quickly and easily, which can lead to unnecessary delays and increased risk to personal safety. Despite the availability of traditional first aid resources, many individuals lack an accessible, user-friendly digital platform that offers interactive tutorials and emergency guidance tailored to real-life scenarios. The Keep Going First Aid app addresses this gap by providing an intuitive and reliable mobile solution that empowers users to handle emergencies confidently, ensuring essential first aid knowledge is available at their fingertips when they need it most.
